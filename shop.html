RECIPE

Citrus Jam 1 cup of sugar / 3 cups of orange and grapefruit segments / 2 tbsp of lime juice / 1/3rd cup of vermouth blanc Take a large mixing bowl and squeeze lemon juice on it. Mix the sugar and the orange and grapefruit segments and let it sit for around half an hour. Put the mixture in a saucepan and add the vermouth. Keep on heating everything until it starts boiling. Make sure that the heat dissolves all the ingredients together and that it feels like a thick paste (like jam). Roughly, it will take 15 minutes for all the ingredients to get dissolved, transfer it into the jar and let the jam cool.
