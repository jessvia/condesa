An Ode to La CondesA

Named after La Condesa, a leafy oasis in the heart of México City where ivy climbs over art deco buildings and tree-lined avenues lead to sun-drenched street corners. Where old meets new, calm meets vibrance, shade meets dappled sunlight. El hogar de la Condesa.
